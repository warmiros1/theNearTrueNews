NT Football Player Breaks Ankles Saving Manti Teo's Girlfriend and Josh Shaw's Nephew

September 12, 2014

By: Jack Sullivan

Photo by: Tor Camorlinga

New Trier Football Player is being hailed as a hero! Patrick Graham broke both his ankles Sunday morning and will unfortunately be out for the rest of the season. In a press conference Monday, Graham explained through several deep gulps of water the story of how he got his injuries. He claimed to be taking a ferry around Lake Michigan when it blew up. Graham was with Josh Shaw's nephew and Manti Teo's girlfriend at the time. Josh Shaw's nephew was recently rescued from drowning in a pool and Manti Teo's girlfriend was tragically diagnosed with terminal leukemia. Graham explained, "As the boat exploded I grabbed Teo's girlfriend and Shaw's nephew with all my strength, which is a lot, because I lift. A piece of shrapnel from the boat shot into my left ankle and that's how it broke."

A Near True News reporter then asked, "How did you break the right ankle?" Graham stood there thinking for a good six minutes and then said that as he swam to shore, half-drowning, he was involved in a brutal shark attack that bit through his foot and broke his right ankle. "I thought that there aren’t any sharks in Lake Michigan?" another reporter asked. "Well, uh, it was actually in the Shedd Aquarium, and I was shot three times." Graham muttered.

The police have now gotten involved in the case because of the attempted murder of Graham while performing such a heroic feat. Graham told the police that it was Wes Welker who shot him. In a response to this, an ESPN reporter asked Graham why Wes Welker was at the Shedd Aquarium, and why he would shoot Graham. Graham replied, "He was in a pretty non-lucid state of mind, ya know?"
