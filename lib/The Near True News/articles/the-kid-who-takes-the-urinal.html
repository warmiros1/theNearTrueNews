August 22, 2014

By: Jack Sullivan

Hello my name Georgey. It’s 5th period and I am walking into the 3rd floor bathroom. It’s empty but I see one kid standing at the urinal near the corner. All urinals are open but I choose the one next to him. He seems lonely. I hear my pitter patter against the porcelain. The kid next to me has not relieved himself yet. I bet he has stage fright due to me staring at him. He turns and I lock eyes with him, he quickly turns away. It has been 2 minutes now but it has gone so quickly. You what they say, “Urine flies when you’re having fun.” This kid next to me feels intimidated by me doing my business next to him. He needs to be comforted. I put my hand out and place it on his back. I rub his back, feeling him sweat out of nervousness. He says “Dude what are you doing? Get away from me.” He zippers up and walks over to a stall. He still needs to relieve himself so he locks the door. Finally he is in peace. It has been 5 minutes now. I hear the drip drop hit the water of the toilet. I climb under the stall door undetected and pop up. I stand there next to him and hold his hand. This has been a slow Tuesday for me.
