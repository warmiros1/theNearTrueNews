Student Hailed as Deity after Plugging in USB Correctly on First Try

August 22, 2014

By: William Armiros

Sources confirmed that sophomore Brian Terry managed to successfully plug in a USB drive into its port on his very first try. This scientific phenomenon has yet to be explained and so, naturally is being called a miracle or some act of God himself.

As we all know, USB ports require at the very least two, if not three or four attempts to accurately plug it in. Usually these attempts include trying to plug it in one way, then trying to plug it in the other way, then checking the USB itself only to discover you were right the first time.

Witnesses describe the scene as dramatic to say the least. Sophomore Michael Jones says she was doing her homework when the miraculous event transpired and claims, “for a second, just one second, I swear the light of a billion suns burst from that universal serial bus port. It was beautiful.”

Terry’s girlfriend is ecstatic with her sudden moment in the spotlight. Never leaving Brian's side, she said, “You know I was planning on breaking up with Brian since he was kind of a deadbeat. But now, he's like a whole new man--no. A whole new God. I'm totally devoted to him now. And what's more, boy am I going to be famous! I mean my name will be up there with Jackie Kennedy and the Virgin Mary!”

Terry himself is rather shocked that he was chosen for this divine honor, and he is certainly reaping the benefits. At press time, he has locked himself in the silent study room on a throne of iPads (don’t worry, they’re 3rd gen) to impart his knowledge onto what he has referred to as his chosen disciples.
