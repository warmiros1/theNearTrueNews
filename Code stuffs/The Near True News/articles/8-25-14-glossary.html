Week of August 25 Glossary

August 25, 2014

iPad: A pretty good way to indicate status among New Trier students. The kids with the iPad Airs are the “in” crowd. 3rd and 4th gens can have normal lives, but they are at a significant disadvantage to their peers. 2nd gen kids? Well, they might as well enroll at ETHS.


Schedule: Just about the worst thing you’ve ever seen in your entire life. I mean five periods with that gross kid that spends so much time near you that you think some of his stench is rubbing off onto you? Who’s making these things anyways?


Schedule Change: Undoubtedly the best thing you’ve ever seen in your entire life. Goodbye Jimmy-showers-once-a-week and hello Sarah-smells-like-Victoria’s-Secret.


Syllabus:Turning this baby in signed is just about the only 100% you can hope to get in AP European History. Don’t screw it up.


Academic Integrity: THEY KNOW EVERYTHING. If your friend texts you asking for the answers to number 6, you mustn't respond. They have eyes everywhere and one slip up, one friendly hint on the math homework, and you've landed yourself a one-way ticket to the adviser chair.


Adviser Chair: Regularly performs public lashings, flaying, and (my personal favorite) tarring and feathering to misbehaving or unruly students to send a message. It also makes for decent entertainment, if you’re into that kind of stuff.
