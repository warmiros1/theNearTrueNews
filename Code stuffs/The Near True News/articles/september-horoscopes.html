September Horoscopes

August 22, 2014

Aquarius (January 20 to February 18):Don’t trust your geoscience lab partner’s answers. He’s one of those kids who pretend to know what’s going on in class even though he still hasn’t found out he should be in the room across the hall for chemistry.

Pisces (February 19 to March 20):When the full moon appears in the sky, you and your friend will find yourselves in the middle of a shady drug deal with the North Shore Cartel. The police will arrive. Do not heed your friend’s advice to “just be cool man, be cool.” Get out of there.

Aries (March 21 to April 19):Next lunch you buy from the cafeteria may or may not contain fecal matter. You’re going to have to roll the dice on this one.

Taurus (April 20 to May 20):Next time you use the third stall from the left on the second floor guy’s bathroom, look up. Doesn’t it totally look like a plane?

Gemini (May 21 to June 20):You will fall in love with a tall, dark stranger. He will fall in love with your mother. Madcap hijinks ensue.

Cancer (June 21 to July 22):Your suspicions are correct. Oopsy, the clown you hired for your son’s birthday party, is a serial killer. Keep it a secret, because Oopsy can twist balloon animals the way Picasso drew paintings, and you don’t want your son to be disappointed.

Leo (July 23 to August 22): You will soon be faced with a decision to access google docs via Internet Explorer or turn in your paper a day late. You know exactly what to choose.

Virgo (August 23 to September 22):Start reading necromancy books and stock up on garlic. You will find it strangely relevant this month.

Libra (September 23 to October 22):You’re gonna tell this amazing Chicken and the Road joke. It’s so good that you’ll be receiving non-stop high fives for the rest of the day as you spread your comedy genius. Your celebration will come to an end, however, when you tell your joke to Jeffery, whose pet rooster has recently been run over by a semi-truck. You’ll do your best to mend the irreparable damage you’ve done to his psyche.

Scorpio (October 23 to Novermber 21):You will drop your phone down three flights of the P stairwell and it will land on a sophomore’s head. Don’t worry, the phone is fine.

Sagittarius (November 22 to December 21):You’ll finally have the confidence to accomplish whatever you put your mind to. You can finally go to Space Camp, or swear on national television. Whatever you want really,do you think I care? If you do, then you aren’t ready for this undertaking.

Capricorn (December 22 to January 19):Don’t take creative cuisine sophomore year. It ends with heartbreak, and just a pinch of thyme.
